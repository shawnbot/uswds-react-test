Standards + React

This entire page is a test of event delegation to solve the problem of integrating the Standards' interactive components with React. This is what you should be able to do here:

  • Click the "Here's how you know" button at the very top of the page, and it should expand to reveal more content below. Clicking it again should close it.
  • Click the Search "link" in the header, and the actual search form should appear, then focus. Click anywhere outside of the search form, and it should return to showing the link-like button.
  • Click the navigation dropdowns in the header, and they should collapse (then expand again) on subsequent clicks. No more than one should be expanded at the same time.
  • Shrink your window or view on a mobile device, click the "Menu" button at the top right to open the navigation mobile menu. The close button should get focus, and clicking on it or the page overlay should dismiss the navigation.

React-friendly accordions

  • Open the accordion below, then click "Add an item" to re-render the app with an additional accordion section. Section should remain open whenever you add an item unless you close it again.
  • Click "Start over" to return to the initial state, in which the first accordion should remain open.

Bonus The above accordion also has aria-multiselectable="true", which allows accordions to have multiple sections open at once.
